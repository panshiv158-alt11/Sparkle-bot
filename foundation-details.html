Foundation Courses

Master the basics of robotics with our carefully designed Foundation courses. Perfect for beginners to build strong fundamentals.

🤖 RoboChamp

Age: 5-7 years

An introductory course designed for the youngest learners. Kids explore basic robotics concepts through fun, interactive activities.

What You'll Learn:

  • Basic robotics fundamentals
  • Problem-solving through play
  • Hand-eye coordination and motor skills
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Creative thinking and imagination

Benefits:

  • ✨ Engaging, play-based learning
  • ✨ Builds confidence in STEM
  • ✨ Develops communication skills
  • ✨ Age-appropriate challenges

💻 Scratch Programming

Age: 7-9 years

Learn visual programming with Scratch! Create your own games, animations, and interactive stories while understanding coding logic.

What You'll Learn:

  • Visual programming basics
  • Creating games and animations
  • Logic and algorithmic thinking
  • Debugging and problem-solving
  • Interactive storytelling

Benefits:

  • ✨ No prior coding experience needed
  • ✨ Fun, creative projects
  • ✨ Develops computational thinking
  • ✨ Portfolio-building projects

🎨 Creative Design

Age: 8-10 years

Combine art and robotics! Design and build creative robotic structures that move and interact with the environment.

What You'll Learn:

  • Design thinking methodology
  • Building mechanical structures
  • Integration of art and robotics
  • Prototyping and iteration
  • Material science basics

Benefits:

  • ✨ Encourages creative expression
  • ✨ Hands-on building experience
  • ✨ Develops spatial reasoning
  • ✨ Merges STEM with arts

🧩 Problem Solving

Age: 9-11 years

Master critical thinking through robotics challenges. Solve complex problems by building and programming robots to complete real-world tasks.

What You'll Learn:

  • Advanced problem-solving strategies
  • Complex robot programming
  • Sensor integration and logic
  • Project management basics
  • Real-world application design

Benefits:

  • ✨ Develops critical thinking
  • ✨ Prepares for advanced courses
  • ✨ Builds confidence in STEM
