About Sparklebots

Empowering young innovators with hands-on robotics and future-ready STEM skills.

Since 2009, Sparklebots is a hands-on Robotics and technology learning platform for students aged 6 to 20, focused on building Future-ready skills. We empower young minds through practical learning in Robotics, AI, Coding, and Drone Technology. Learn, Build, and Innovate with real-world projects at Sparklebots.
